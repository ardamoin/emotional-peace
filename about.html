About Me

Welcome to Emotional Peace Psychotherapy

Therapy is in Session.

Welcome! I'm Semra, a Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying) with the CRPO and a member of CCPA. I hold a Masters (M.A.) in Counselling Psychology from Yorkville University and an undergraduate degree in Pharmaceutical Sciences.

I offer online psychotherapy sessions to clients across Ontario via video and telephone. My approach provides a safe space for you to share your story, understand your current situation, and identify your goals. I'm passionate about guiding you on your self-growth journey and providing the tools to achieve your objectives.

I tailor therapeutic modalities and techniques to your unique needs, drawing from an integrative and holistic perspective. I believe in treating each individual with respect and empathy, using approaches such as solution-focused, strength-based, compassion-focused, person-centred, trauma-focused, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), and narrative therapy.

I recognize the importance of addressing mental health issues and the impact they can have on physical well-being. In this space, all aspects of your being are welcome, and it's okay to express a range of emotions without judgment.
